About me


I was born in Belarus and grew up in Israel. After high school I studied for 2 years in college and got a diploma in computer engineering. During college I was selected for a military program and was working on a project for the Air Force through my second year.

I completed 3 years of military service working with electronics in a R&D team. We were planning, building, installing and supporting our systems for the military and other civilian organizations. Most of the time I was working with ditigal hardware and coding in VHDL.

In 2016 I moved to Canada and a year later I started computer engineering in the University of Waterloo. During my work terms and free time I work on embedded and software projects, make music and enjoy sports. I'm hardworking, social and I speak 3 languages.
